# Portfolio Content Writing

Use this skill when updating portfolio copy, case studies, or service positioning.

## Tone

- Clear, confident, and technical without sounding inflated.
- Business-aware rather than buzzword-heavy.
- Specific about workflows and outcomes.

## Content rules

- Do not reveal confidential client names or private internal details.
- Prefer anonymized descriptions such as `moving company CRM`.
- Highlight problem, solution, and business impact.
- Keep claims grounded in what was actually built.

## Structure guidance

- Home: positioning, credibility, featured work, services, CTA
- About: approach, strengths, collaboration style
- Projects: representative outcomes and technical range
- Case studies: context, challenge, implementation, impact
